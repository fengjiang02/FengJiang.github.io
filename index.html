Biography

I am a third-year PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Texas at Arlington. I received my M.S. in Software Engineering from UTA (2021-2022) and B.E. in Software Engineering from Xi'an Jiaotong University City College (2015-2019).


Research

My research interests include: - Large Foundation Models & Multimodal Large Language Models - Graph Neural Networks - Deep AUC Maximization - Long-tailed Learning
